How Gift Cards Work

We currently support only Amazon E-Gift Cards. Buy a gift card from Amazon and paste the code in the form above. Only a working gift card gets rewarded.

  1. Go to the Amazon Gift Cards page (official site).
  2. Choose E-Gift Card (Email) and select a preset value or enter a custom amount.
  3. Enter the recipient email (your email) to receive the gift card.
  4. Optionally, add a personal message like: "Support Photon Studios ‚Äì Thank you!"
  5. Checkout and complete the payment using your Amazon account.
  6. After purchase, copy the digital gift card code (something like ABCD-EFGH-IJKL).
  7. Go to the donation form above, fill out your username and email, and paste the gift card code.
  8. Submit the form. You will receive a suitable reward via email once the code is redeemed.

Make sure you check your email daily (even spam/promotions) to not miss your rewards!
