Who We Are

Photon Studios is an independent game development team driven by a shared passion for creating immersive and innovative interactive experiences. Founded by a group of enthusiasts who love science fiction, complex puzzles, and compelling narratives, we aim to craft unique games that resonate with players long after the credits roll.

We operate with a core belief in quality over quantity, focusing on deeply polished projects that push the boundaries of what games can achieve.

Our Current Focus

Our primary project is Portal: The Last Dance, a fan-made continuation exploring the beloved Portal universe.

  • Goal: To deliver a high-quality, story-driven puzzle experience honoring the original while introducing new challenges.
  • Features: New characters, innovative mechanics, and a compelling narrative.
  • Status: Actively in development.

We are also constantly exploring concepts for future projects, seeking opportunities to experiment with new genres and technologies.

Our Approach

We believe in the power of games as an art form. Our development process emphasizes iterative design, player feedback, and technical excellence. We strive to create games that are not only fun but also thought-provoking and emotionally resonant.

Collaboration and open communication are key within our team, ensuring that every member's voice is heard and contributes to the final product.
